Eggs Benedict

Eggs Benedict requires a bit more effort than the sourdough waffle recipe, but if you have some time to spare in the morning this recipe is well worth the effort and becomes fairly easy after you've done it a few times.

Hollandaise Ingredients:

  • 3 egg yokes

  • 8 tablespoons of butter

  • 2 teaspoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice

  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

  • A pinch of cayenne pepper

Hollandaise Sauce Recipe

  1. Start simmering 1 inch of water in a medium saucepan. Reduce heat to low

  2. In a medium mixing bowl, whisk the egg yokes until they lighten in color

  3. Place the bowl over the medium saucepan and whisk continuously until thickened, at least 3 minutes

  4. Remove from heat and slowly add melted butter, once tablespoon at a time, whisking to incorporate.

  5. Once all the butter is added, add your remaining ingredients. Use within 30 minutes!

Eggs Benedict Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • 8 slices canadian bacon

  • 4 toasted english muffins with butter

  • 8 poached eggs (figure it out yourself!)

  • Kosher salt and black pepper

  • Hollandaise sauce

Eggs Benedict Recipe:

  1. Melt the butter over medium high heat, once foamy add the canadian bacon and let cook until done, turning as needed

  2. Place your warm english muffins on a plate, adding more butter if needed

  3. Place a slice of canadian bacon on each english muffin slice

  4. On top of the canadian bacon put a poached egg, followed by a generous amount of hollandaise, some pepper and garnish if desired

  5. Serve! Recommend serving with some crispy roasted potatos, go great with any extra hollandaise on your plate
